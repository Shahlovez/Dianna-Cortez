For Classes, Workshops, Warm-ups, Vocal/Dialect Coaching for Productions, and Private Lessons we will:

  • Identify and release habitual tension in the body and mind
  • Freeing the Breath
  • Connect YOU to your voice
  • Strengthen and Embody your Vibrations
  • Eliminate Jaw, Tongue, and Soft-Palate/Throat Tension
  • Re-elasticize Ribs and the Reflex action in you Diaphragm for the agility to respond to different sized thoughts and feelings
  • Access a 3-4 Octave Speaking Range as needed for heightened expression
  • Speak with clarity through energized Articulation
  • Phonetics and how Vowels and Consonants are formed in the mouth and are experienced in the body
  • Individual Dialect Analysis of How you speak
  • World Dialects and Embodying how Others may speak
  • Personalizing Text and Enlivening Poetry, Songs, Monologues, and/or Speeches
  • Acting and Singing with Extended Voice and Movement through Imaginative play
  • Song Interpretation
  • Acting Coaching
  • For Depth, Clarity, and Presence
